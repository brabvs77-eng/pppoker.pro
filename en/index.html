🎲 Russian Poker is now live in the 🤝NUTS club!

🔥 A fresh, high-octane format for players who crave more excitement—here, you don’t play against the table, but head-to-head against the dealer!

💡 Game Rules:

1️⃣ First, place an Ante bet and receive 5 cards.

2️⃣ You can fold, place a bet, exchange cards, or even buy a 6th card by paying an additional Ante.

3️⃣ The dealer also receives 5 cards and reveals one at random. The hand only qualifies if the dealer’s final hand is at least Ace-King or better.

4️⃣ If your hand beats the dealer’s qualifying hand—you win according to the payout table.

5️⃣ If the dealer doesn’t qualify (i.e., has “no game”), you keep your bets and receive a bonus!

📊 Chips & Strategy Tips:

• Start playing only with a pair or stronger. If you hold only unconnected, low cards without an Ace or King—fold almost every time.

• Buying a 6th card is worthwhile only if you already have four cards to a flush or straight. Don’t waste money chasing miracle outs like gutshot straights.

• Always remember the Ace-King rule: even with a modest hand, you can still win if the dealer fails to qualify!

✨ Final Thoughts Russian Poker is a fantastic way to spice up your standard poker experience and see the game from a fresh perspective! 💪

В PPPOKER есть два вида джекпотов

Cooler

If players reveal four-in-one versus four-in-one or higher cards on the showdown (the so-called Cooler situation), all participants in the hand, including those who discarded the cards, will receive a share of the Cooler jackpot.

Distribution of prizes:
The loser -50%
Winner - 30%
Other players -20%.

2

The older hand

(Only for "Hold'em") If by the end of the hand a player has a combination of four cards or higher in his hands, he and all the players who participated in the hand (including those who folded) will receive their share of the jackpot won in the largest hand.
